About

Let me introduce myself.

I love to make things.


As a child, I only had crazy ideas - but no means to make it happen.

Therefore, through my exploration of avenues to create, I decided to double major in Studio Arts and Computer Science.

Since then, I have been actively pushing the intersection of art and technology via focus on user centered products.

I founded flyerApp.events. to make events on campus easier to manage for students.
I developed spirofy.com to make music something visual for Spotify users
I lead the development of finternz.com to make internships easier to find and apply to.

I am passionate about making a change, and leveraging my skills in software and art to make it happen.
